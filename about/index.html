ABOUT

Ryan Summer, a seasoned music producer and audio engineer based in LA, traces his roots back to Long Island, NY, where he was born, & Fort Lauderdale, FL where he grew up. His journey into the music industry began in 2007 when he first delved into making and selling beats, online and to local talent. With clients seeking not only his production prowess but also his expertise in the entire creative process, Ryan then started lending his tastes to the recording, editing, and mixing process.

In 2011, Ryan seized an opportunity to intern at a local recording studio, immersing himself in the intricacies of the trade, forging valuable connections, and learning the business side of music. By 2014, Ryan opened his own commercial production studio, regularly hosting production and songwriting sessions, as well as providing recording and mixing services for his clients.

The year 2015 marked a significant shift as Ryan relocated to Los Angeles to collaborate with major label artists. This move placed him in elite circles alongside top songwriters, producers, and music executives, further enriching his professional network and artistic tastes.

In 2018, Ryan's expertise in mixing and mastering caught the attention of major international artists, expanding his client base and solidifying his reputation in the industry. Today, Ryan dedicates himself full-time to mixing, mastering and producing, occasionally lending his engineering and vocal production skills to select artists when time permits.

Some notable songs Ryan has contributed to are, Saweetie & Doja Cat's "Best Friend", which was Grammy-nominated for Best Rap Song in 2022, Saweetie's remix of her massive hit "Tap In", Jason Derulo's remix of the viral TikTok sensation, "Jiggle Jiggle" by Louis Theroux & Duke & Jones, and most recently, JVKE’s “This Is What Forever Feels Like” feat. Nick Jonas.
